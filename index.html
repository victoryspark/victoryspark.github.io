The world's most powerful process for starting a business.

Create

Finding a busienss model is like finding a needle in a hay stack. Its a daunting task but with the right equipment it can be done. We guide you through hwo to create discrete and measurable experiments to test the vailidity of your business model. Entrepreneurship is melting pot of science and art.

Iterate

Odds are your first set of hypotheses are wrong, that's okay! We'll help you take the data you've collected on a weekly basis to modify your business model hypotheses. By the end of the program you'll have a sharp shooting business model with incredible focus on your customer segment, value proposition, and how you're going to generate revenue.

Validate

Once you connect a customer segment, value proposition, and revenue we are on the pathway to product-market-fit. We'll help you prepare to exploit these validations to create a repeatable and scaleable process to begin growing your business.

Sales and Marketing is not the gun slinging of the West many think it is.

Its a process that takes time and patience to development. But once its repeatable, you can begin to grow and train your team to effective closers.

Sales

Sales starts with the CEO and is driven across the organization in the early days. It is critical that every member of the team understands the sales cycle, customer segment, and the value they are buying. There are no employees that wear only one hat - everyone must be selling!

Marketing

Storytelling is imperative to the success of reaching and finding out why customers care. A good story lets customers understand why you exist and why you are better than everyone else.

Minimum Viable Product

No prototype? No worries. We'll work with you through the Customer Development process to help you first define what your customers find important and build a minimumially viable product around it.

Whether you can build software or just wireframes, can ducktape a prototype together in your basement, the MVP is meant to be down and dirtt and cheap. The MVP is ever evolving and as the founder you must be divorsed from the product and rather be married to your customer value propositions.

Prototyping and Small Scale Production

Victory Sparks works with several local partners to offer prototyping capabilities and small scale production facilities. Depending on your needs we can cater personal introductions to you to some of the best and talented builders in our community.
